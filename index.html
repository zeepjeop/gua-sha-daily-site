Questionsv.

Honest answers.

Is Gua Sha Daily free?

Your first 14-day journey is free, including the daily photo, the guided morning ritual, your streak, and the Day-14 reveal. Plus unlocks every routine and unlimited journeys — $3.99/week, $24.99/year, or $49.99 once for lifetime.

Where do my photos go?

Nowhere. They're encrypted with AES-256 and stored only inside the app's private space on your phone — never your camera roll, never a server, never a cloud backup. If you delete the app, they're gone. The only photo that can ever leave is a share card you explicitly tap Share on.

Do I have to take a photo every day?

No — the photo is optional, though it's the heart of the reveal: each one adds a frame to your before & after. Miss a day and nothing breaks; the ritual simply continues.

Will gua sha change my skin?

We don't make promises about results — and we'd gently suggest being wary of apps that do. Gua sha is a traditional practice many people find calming and pleasant. Our job is simpler: help you keep a gentle habit for two weeks and show you your own Day 1 and Day 14, side by side. What you see there is yours to judge.

Does the app scan or rate my face?

No. There is no face detection, no AI analysis, and no scoring of any kind. The camera's alignment guide is a simple static overlay that helps your photos line up — nothing more.

What do I need to start?

A gua sha stone (any shape you like), a few drops of facial oil, and about five quiet minutes. The app includes a gentle how-to for beginners and an oil guide matched to your skin type.

How do I cancel?

Two taps in your App Store or Google Play subscription settings — the same place you manage every other subscription. No emails to write, no retention screens. Lifetime is a one-time purchase with nothing to cancel.

Is this medical advice?

No. Gua Sha Daily is a guided self-care practice, not a medical or cosmetic treatment. Move gently, stop if anything feels uncomfortable, and speak to a professional about any skin or health concerns.
