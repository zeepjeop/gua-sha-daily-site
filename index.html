FAQ

Fair questions

Is it really private?

Yes — by design, not policy. Photos are AES-256 encrypted on your device, excluded from cloud backups, and there is no server that receives them. No account, no face scanning, ever. The only photo that can leave is a share card you explicitly tap Share on.

What happens after the free week?

Your daily photo, streak, core routine and full 14-day journey stay free forever. The full routine library and unlimited journeys need Plus — priced exactly as shown above, with no hidden trial-to-charge tricks. We never take a payment method for the free week.

Do you promise results?

No. Gua sha is a calm self-care practice, and we make no claims about what it changes. Nothing is measured or scored. The app gives you two honest photos, two weeks apart — what you see there is yours to judge.
